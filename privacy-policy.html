01 Introduction

Welcome to Balagha ("we," "our," or "us"). We are an Indian modest fashion brand dedicated to crafting premium prayer dresses and nightwear for women. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with any part of it, please discontinue use of our site.

02 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account or place an order
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us with enquiries or support requests
  • Participate in promotions or provide feedback

The types of personal information we collect include:

  • Identity data: Full name, username, or similar identifier
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, billing and delivery address
  • Transaction data: Details about payments and purchases made
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies
  • Usage data: Information about how you use our website and services

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders, including shipping and payment
  • Communicating with you about your orders, returns, and enquiries
  • Sending promotional emails and offers, only if you have opted in
  • Improving our website, products, and customer experience
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring the security of our platform
  • Complying with legal obligations under Indian law
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

04 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies — small data files placed on your device — to improve your browsing experience. We use cookies to:

  • Remember your cart and wishlist between sessions
  • Understand how visitors use our site (analytics)
  • Personalise content and recommendations

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website such as the cart and wishlist.

05 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your personal data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services, including:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, PhonePe, Paytm) for secure transactions
  • Shipping partners (India Post, DTDC, Delhivery) for order fulfilment
  • Email service providers for transactional and marketing communication
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) to understand website usage

All third parties are required to respect the security of your personal data and treat it in accordance with applicable law. We do not allow them to use your data for their own purposes.

06 Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organisational security measures designed to protect your personal information from accidental loss, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. All payment information is encrypted using SSL technology.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

07 Your Rights

As a customer, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@balagha.in.
