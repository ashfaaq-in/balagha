Shipping & Returns

Home / Shipping & Returns
Last updated: April 2026

01 Shipping Timeline & Processing

At Balagha, we take pride in carefully preparing your order for safe delivery. Our general shipping timeline is as follows:

  • Processing Time: Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days. Custom or made-to-order items may require an additional 3-5 days.
  • Standard Delivery (India): 4-7 business days from the date of dispatch, depending on the destination state and accessibility.
  • Express Delivery (Metro Cities): 2-4 business days for major Indian metro cities when expedited shipping is selected at checkout.
Please note that during major festive seasons or sales events, delivery times may be slightly extended due to high courier volumes.

02 Shipping Charges

We believe in transparent pricing without hidden surprises:

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above ₹2,000 across India.
  • Standard Shipping Fee: A flat rate of ₹100 applies to orders below ₹2,000.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): An extra ₹50 convenience fee applies to all COD orders regardless of the order value.

03 Return Eligibility

We want you to love everything you order from Balagha. If something isn't right, we accept returns under the following conditions:

  • The return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their pristine original condition.
  • All original tags and packaging must be intact and attached.
  • For hygiene reasons, innerwear, custom-made items, and accessories cannot be returned or exchanged.

04 How to Return or Exchange

Initiating a return or exchange is simple and straightforward:

  • Email us at hello@balagha.in with your Order ID and the reason for the return.
  • Once approved, our courier partner will pick up the item from your delivery address within 2-3 business days.
  • Please securely pack the item exactly as you received it to prevent any transit damage.

In case a reverse pickup service is not available for your pincode, we may request you to self-ship the item via a reliable courier, and we will reimburse up to ₹100 towards shipping costs.

05 Refunds & Store Credit

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund:

  • Prepaid Orders: Refunds are credited directly back to the original source of payment within 5-7 business days of approval.
  • COD Orders: Refunds for Cash on Delivery orders are issued exclusively in the form of Balagha Store Credit (Gift Card) valid for 6 months.
Shipping charges and the COD convenience fee are strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

06 Damaged or Defective Items

Quality is our priority. In the rare event that you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. We will arrange an immediate replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.
